ABOUT

MRU Coding Club is run by the student committee and is open to all who are keenly interested in coding and programming. Our club will give exposure to real-life problems and to various coding contests, help students to ace their knowledge in the right direction. Our student committee will be available for support and guidance thereby enhancing technical skills.
